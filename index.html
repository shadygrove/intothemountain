--- title: Welcome ---

During the summer of 2014, seeking fellowship and great music, some friends started meeting together to jam and share songs. Then, one of them said, "hey we should record that.", and... so they did.

Into The Mountain is not just a band, but a story about friends loving God, loving music and a simply having a great time... together.

All of us play music in the Murrysville Community Church worship team. We talked often about getting together to play music "for fun", and one day we finally made it happen. It turned out to be some of the best times of laughter and joy... and we couldn't stop.

The original band is...

  • Dave Reed - lead vocals, guitar, cigar box guitar
  • Eddie Agate - lead vocals, background vocals, guitar, bass
  • Scott Lentz - mandolin, accordion, banjolin
  • Eric Bowser - percussion (box drum, tambourine, washboard, and anything you can bang on)
  • Martin Bour - banjo, guitar, and production engineer

We have also had the honor of working with Russ Norris on jazz guitar and Sara Benis on violin.
